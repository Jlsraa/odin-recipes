Curry

Description

This is a japanese curry, sweet, savory and rich.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Curry powder
  • Garam masala
  • Boneless chicken tighs
  • Chicken stock
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Curry Bullion
  • Ketchup
  • Soy sauce
  • Worshestershire sauce
  • Parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Add oil to a pot and add thinly sliced onions to cook.
  2. Once the onion looks transparent, add finely chopped garlic clove, curry powder and garam masala. Cook until fragrant.
  3. Cut the chicken tighs into bite sized pieces and add them to the pot to sear. Add a pinch of salt.
  4. When the outer part of the chicken lookes cooked, add diced potatoes, diced carrots and chicken stock.
  5. Let it boil and lower the heat to let simmer. Let it cook until the potatoes and carrots are soft.
  6. Add ketchup, Worshestershire sauce and soy sauce and incorporate.
  7. Add curry bullion and let it thicken.
  8. Serve with cooked sticky rice on the side, garnish with finely chopped parsley and enjoy.
