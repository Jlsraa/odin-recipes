Cauliflower Pizza

Description

This pizza crust is made from a cauliflower, eggs and cheese. Perfect when looking for a low carb meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cauliflower head
  • ½ Cup of shredded cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • Tomato Sauce
  • ¼ Cup Shredded cheese
  • 3 Sliced Mushrooms
  • Pepperoni
  • Thinly sliced red onion
  • Goat cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Cut the cauliflower head into tiny pieces, add them to a food processor until smaller pieces form.
  3. Add the coliflcauliflowerower pieces to a cheesecloth and squeeze to get all the moisture out.
  4. Add the squeezed coliflower to a bowl with the half cup of shredded cheese, egg, a pinch of salt and mix until everithing is well combined.
  5. Add the mixture to a sheet pan and cook it in the oven for 15 minutes.
  6. Take the sheet pan out and flip the crust, add it to the oven once more to cook and crisp por another 15 minutes.
  7. Take the crust out and add all your toppings.
  8. Pop the pizza back in the oven for 10 minutes to broil and crisp.
