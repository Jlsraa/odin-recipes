Red Chilaquiles

Description

Comforting and delicious, chilaquiles are a traditional mexican breakfast. Make them as soft or as crunchy as you like.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Guajillo chiles
  • Chiles de arbol
  • Chicken stock or chicken bullion
  • Oregano
  • Totopos
  • Cream
  • Cotija Cheese
  • Cilantro leaves

Steps

  1. Char guajillo and arbol chilles, avoid to burn them completely.
  2. Add onion, garlic, tomatoes, chiles and the chicken stock and blend until a sauce is formed.
  3. Heat a pot, add oil and when hot, pour the sauce in the pan.
  4. Let the sauce boil, then add oregano and turn down the heat to let it simmer for 5 minutes and then turn off the heat.
  5. Add the desired ammount of totopos to the sauce, fish them out and add them to a plate.
  6. Top with cream, cotija cheese and cilantro leaves and enjoy!
